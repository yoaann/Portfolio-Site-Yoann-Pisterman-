"Note" taking app

Approach

01

“note” is a note-taking mobile app. Its look and design should be defined by Functionalism principles based on the industrial designer Dieter Rams. “How things look and their design should be defined by their function.”

02

The interface design is based on iOS Human interface guideline, in order to understand the UI patterns, iconography and navigation intuitively.

03

Sans Serif Font Verdana is used for the text input and helps for a clear readability, avoiding reading tiredness and helps people with dyslexia.

04

Containers with soft edges and gray background for an ergonomic feel, smooth contrast and ease the eyes.
