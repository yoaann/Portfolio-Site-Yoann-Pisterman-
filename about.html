About

My story

I’m Yoann Pisterman, Berlin based designer, visual artist and music composer.

As designer I’m focusing on User interface design, Brand & identity design. I have a visual, problem solving and human centric design approach. I seek for a balance between clean and meaningful outcome. Proactive and dedicated, I aim to build open and informal collaborations to reach mutual ambitions. As artist I have the propensity for auditory and visual representation of neo-impressionist architectural landscapes. In my free time, I like to do things like cooking french and indian dishes, hiking in order ressource myslef in the nature, traveling to refresh my perceptiion and understandings…
